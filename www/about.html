About

What is the Resolution Project:

A year-long project that tracks 9 participants and their 2017 New Year's resolutions.

Process:

At the end of each month in 2017, an email is sent out with questions to track the participants' resolution progress. There are three sets of questions: beginning, middle, and end.

The "beginning" set, sent in January, asks the participants to declare a resolution.

The "middle" set, sent February through November, track resolution progress, changes to schedule, and feelings towards resolution completion.

The "end" set, sent in December, provides a year end checkpoint on the status of the resolution.

Effort of participants:

  • Answer 5-6 questions each month for the next 12 months.
  • Be okay with me sharing first names*, ages, professional fields, and any information about their resolutions.

* Some names were changed

Who are the participants:

The participants are people I know who I could reliably get in contact with each month.

Who am I?

I am Kelly Devine, a front-end developer living in New York, New York.

Motivation for The Resolution Project:

Two reasons —

  1. I am super fascinated by the idea of a New Year resolution. We can decide at any point of the year to make a change in our lives, but the New Year resolution is so powerful. It's the start of a New Year, a new you!
  2. Using this as an opportunity to stretch my brain as a dev and design project.

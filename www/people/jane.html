How do you feel about the idea of New Year Resolutions?

I think it's a nice sentiment towards self improvement.

What is your 2016 Resolution?

I want to start and maintain a bullet journal, ideally working on it several times a week but at minimum once a week. My life isn't that interesting so I can't expect to update it every day and create meaningful content. Mainly it'll give me a chance to practice hand lettering and graphic design/compositions with some direction.

Do you think you will accomplish this resolution by the end of December 2017?

Knowing my past history with starting/completing projects, I'm not optimistic I will. However, this isn't something that needs to be seen publicly, which is less pressure to make perfect. Hopefully that will make it easier to maintain.

Have you outlined steps to accomplish your 2017 New Year Resolution?

Nope!

Has your daily routine changed in any way because of your 2017 goal?

Not terribly.

Has measurable progress been made on achieving your goal?

Yes and no. I think I initially said I wanted to do more journaling to keep my creativity up. So the physical goal of journaling has not progressed (especially since I started the journal in [a recent visit to] Spain and it's just too bittersweet to start again right now), but I have been meeting those creative goals. So I'm not sure whether it's a yes or no in your book!

At the time of this survey, we are nearing the end of the month. Does the time of year bear any weight on your conscience regarding your goal?

Nope.

Do you plan to continue your path towards achieving your 2017 Resolution?

I think so! My goal was to be and produce more creativity and I think I'm doing that.

Does my involvement make any impact on your feelings/resolution progress?

So far, nope!

Have you outlined steps to accomplish your 2017 New Year Resolution?

I changed my approach, but it wasn't an active 'i must do A, B, C, etc.' Essentially instead of bullet journaling, I got a planner (on sale, I couldn't resist haha) that has bullet points for days, and I use that to journal.

Has your daily routine changed in any way because of your 2017 goal?

Not daily. I do think I've added more to my usual free-time activities, but I wouldn't say I changed my routine.

Has measurable progress been made on achieving your goal?

I think so! I finally finished an application to teach a hand-lettering workshop at work, which involved reworking some small lettering pieces, and I have continued to either illustrate or hand-letter to keep up my creative output.

At the time of this survey, we are nearing the end of the month. Does the time of year bear any weight on your conscience regarding your goal?

Not at all! Actually I'm surprised that I've kept up with my resolution, granted it was an abstract one to keep up creative output/energy.

Yes!

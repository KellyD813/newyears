How do you feel about the idea of New Year Resolutions?

As I was sitting down to write this, making my decision on my New Year's resolution I instantly had a bad taste in my mouth. "This stuff is for the people who lack discipline". My next thought was "wow why would I default to such a negative stance on this?"

What is your 2016 Resolution?

Then it became clear. I'm afraid to make demands on myself. Well, here’s to being afraid and going for it. My new year’s resolution is to stop avoiding calls, texts, emails or other forms of communication and to respond in a timely manner. I'd like to say procrastination but it really only pertains to avenues of communication.

Do you think you will accomplish this resolution by the end of December 2017?

I am hopeful I will meet the mark but I do have to admit that this failed goal crosses my mind just about every week. But since I've put it out there, heck... maybe this is my time!

Have you outlined steps to accomplish your 2017 New Year Resolution?

Wow, now I feel lazy. I have not outlined any steps in accomplishing this goal. It tends to be more of a mental hurdle consisting of "just do it now becca". So rather than steps, it's more black and white.

Has your daily routine changed in any way because of your 2017 goal?

I wouldn't say my daily routine has changed, like I said it's mainly a mental game of making a split second decision to do the task right then.

Has measurable progress been made on achieving your goal?

I would say I get back to texts, voice mails, emails in a day or two (except for this lol) rather than my usual weeks.

At the time of this survey, we are nearing the end of the month. Does the time of year bear any weight on your conscience regarding your goal?

Yes, but not because of the new year. I am 9 weeks away from being a mom so I'm striving to be more organized and prompt.

Do you plan to continue your path towards achieving your 2017 Resolution?

Yes! I'm making more and more of an effort. I've realized that the longer I wait to respond, the quicker I forget about doing it at all especially with pregnant brain.

Does my involvement make any impact on your feelings/resolution progress?

Well considering part of this experiment is getting back to you via email, yes! lol although I have to say I'm a little embarrassed that your emails have taken me the longest to respond to. But yes, knowing someone is watching makes the stakes higher.

Considering I don't have any red demon numbers plaguing me on my iPhone screen, I'd say that is measurable! It usually says zero texts and voicemails.

At the time of this survey, we are nearing the end of the month. Does the time of year bear any weight on your conscience regarding your goal?

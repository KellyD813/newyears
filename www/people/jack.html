How do you feel about the idea of New Year Resolutions?

I've generally shied away from them in the past because it always seemed unrealistic to believe that I'd stick to something I set out to do in January. Nowadays, I think that it's important to take chances to set small goals, push your limits, and hold yourself accountable.

What is your 2016 Resolution?

This year, I plan to listen to more music - and make time to really dive into it. Expand my record collection, get a better sound system, find new ways to discover what's out there, etc.

Do you think you will accomplish this resolution by the end of December 2017?

I do think I'll accomplish this resolution this year - mostly because the goal and metrics that define it are pretty vague.

Have you outlined steps to accomplish your 2017 New Year Resolution?

I haven't quite outlined steps to accomplish my resolution as much as I have outlined how to track my progress on my resolution. I've been keeping tabs in a note on my phone.

Has your daily routine changed in any way because of your 2017 goal?

My daily routine hasn't changed but I would say that I actively work toward achieving my goal because I'm trying to hold myself accountable. I listen to more full albums, even if I don't like the music at first. I've read more music blogs. And I've tried to listen to vinyl more often than not.

Has measurable progress been made on achieving your goal?

Well, in the sense that it can be measured? yes. so far this year (since I've been tracking it), I've listened to 15 new albums and 43 albums on vinyl.

At the time of this survey, we are nearing the end of the month. Does the time of year bear any weight on your conscience regarding your goal?

I think it bears a little weight but I'm not too worried about exposing myself to new music. I just think I should pick up the pace a bit.

Do you plan to continue your path towards achieving your 2017 Resolution?

Yes!

Does my involvement make any impact on your feelings/resolution progress?

I think your involvement makes me consider why I made my 2017 resolution and forces me to take more time to work toward my goals.

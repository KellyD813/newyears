How do you feel about the idea of New Year Resolutions?

I'm not too fond of New Year's resolutions, because most of the time you want to achieve something unattainable, then disappointed when you don't accomplish said resolution. I like making small "resolutions" throughout the year that I can achieve.

What is your 2016 Resolution?

Clean out closet and consign belongings every 3 months.

Do you think you will accomplish this resolution by the end of December 2017?

YES! I'm starting already, little by little.

Have you outlined steps to accomplish your 2017 New Year Resolution?

Not necessarily, just knowing that every couple months I need to get rid of clothing.

Has your daily routine changed in any way because of your 2017 goal?

No. Now on my days off I schedule when I'm going to clean out my closet.

Has measurable progress been made on achieving your goal?

No.
